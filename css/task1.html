FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The 2014 FIFA World Cup was held in Brazil from June 12 to July 13, 2014. Venues: The matches were held in 12 different cities across Brazil, including Rio de Janeiro, Sao Paulo, Brasilia, and Salvador. Goal of the Tournament: James Rodríguez also won the Goal of the Tournament award for his spectacular volley against Uruguay in the Round of 16. Fair Play Award: Colombia received the FIFA Fair Play Award for their exemplary fair play during the tournament. Notable Performances: Costa Rica emerged as the surprise package of the tournament, reaching the quarter-finals for the first time in their history. Additionally, the Netherlands showcased an impressive run, finishing in third place. Memorable Moments: The tournament witnessed several memorable moments, including the 7-1 semi-final victory of Germany over Brazil, which was Brazil's worst-ever World Cup defeat.
